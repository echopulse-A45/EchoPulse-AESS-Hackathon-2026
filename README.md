 Overview

EchoPulse is an AI-powered flood monitoring and water management system designed to address flash floods and water scarcity using FMCW radar, IoT sensors, artificial intelligence, and solar-powered infrastructure.

 Objectives

- Reduce flood risks
- Improve early warning systems
- Minimize energy consumption
- Support water reuse
- Enhance environmental monitoring

System Features

- FMCW Radar Monitoring
- IoT Sensor Network
- AI-Based Flood Prediction
- Event-Driven Low-Power Operation
- Smart Alert System
- Water Reuse Management
- Solar-Powered Infrastructure

 AI Technologies

LSTM

- Flood forecasting
- Time-series prediction
- Early warning generation

 Random Forest

- Flood risk classification
- Hazard evaluation
- Decision support

 IoT Monitoring

The system monitors:

- Water level
- Water flow
- pH
- Turbidity
- Temperature

 Alert System

- SMS alerts
- Emergency sirens
- Warning lights
- Mobile notifications

 Water Reuse

The system analyzes water quality using:

- pH
- Turbidity
- Temperature

 Energy Management

- Event-driven radar activation
- Solar energy system
- Backup battery support
- Intelligent energy optimization
