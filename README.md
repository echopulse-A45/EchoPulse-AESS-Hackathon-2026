# 🚀 Project Overview

EchoPulse is an intelligent AI-powered flood monitoring and sustainable water management system designed to address two critical environmental challenges in Jordan:

- 🌊 Flash Floods  
- 💧 Water Scarcity  

The system combines:

- 📡 FMCW Radar Technology  
- 🌐 IoT Smart Sensors  
- 🧠 Artificial Intelligence (LSTM & Random Forest)  
- ⚡ Event-Driven Low-Power Activation  
- ☀️ Solar Energy Infrastructure  
- 💧 Smart Floodwater Reuse

# 🎯 Main Objectives

- Reduce flood risks and damages  
- Save lives through early warnings  
- Minimize energy consumption  
- Reuse floodwater efficiently  
- Support sustainable environmental monitoring  

---

# 💡 Key Innovations

- 🌱 Green Radar System using Event-Driven Activation  
- 🧠 AI Flood Prediction using LSTM  
- 📊 Dynamic Risk Scoring System (0–100)  
- 📡 FMCW Radar + IoT Sensor Network  
- 💧 Intelligent Water Reuse System  
- ☀️ Fully Solar-Powered Infrastructure  
- 🚨 Multi-Layer Emergency Alert System  
- ⚡ Up to 75% Energy Saving  

---

# 🧠 AI Technologies

## LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory)
- Flood forecasting  
- Time-series prediction  
- Early warning (6–24 hours ahead)  

## Random Forest
- Flood risk classification  
- Hazard evaluation  
- Decision support  

---

# 📡 IoT Monitoring System

- Water Level  
- Flow Rate  
- pH  
- Turbidity  
- Temperature  

Radar activates only when abnormal conditions are detected.

---

# 🚨 Smart Alert System

- 📩 SMS Alerts  
- 🚨 Emergency Sirens  
- 💡 Warning Lights  
- 📱 Notifications  

Backup mode works without internet.

---

# 💧 Intelligent Water Reuse

### Water Quality Analysis:
- pH  
- Turbidity  
- Temperature  

### Output:
- ✅ Clean water → irrigation & services  
- ⚠ Polluted water → safe drainage
