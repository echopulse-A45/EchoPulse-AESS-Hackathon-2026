# 🚀 Project Overview

EchoPulse is an intelligent AI-powered flood monitoring and sustainable water management system designed to address two critical environmental challenges in Jordan:

- 🌊 Flash Floods
- 💧 Water Scarcity

The system combines:

- 📡 FMCW Radar Technology
- 🌐 IoT Smart Sensors
- 🧠 Artificial Intelligence (LSTM & Random Forest)
- ⚡ Event-Driven Low-Power Activation
- ☀️ Solar Energy Infrastructure
- 💧 Smart Floodwater Reuse

EchoPulse enables real-time monitoring, early flood prediction, intelligent emergency response, and sustainable reuse of excess water.

# 🧠 AI Technologies

## LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory)
Used for:
- Flood forecasting
- Time-series prediction
- Early warning generation (6–24 hours before flooding)

## Random Forest
Used for:
- Flood risk classification
- Hazard evaluation
- Intelligent decision support

## Future AI Expansion
- Smart camera monitoring
- Vehicle/person/water detection
- Real-time anomaly detection

---

# 📡 IoT Monitoring System

EchoPulse continuously monitors:

- Water Level
- Water Flow
- pH
- Turbidity
- Temperature

The radar activates only when abnormal conditions are detected to reduce power consumption.

---

# 🚨 Smart Alert System

The system provides:

- 📩 SMS Alerts
- 🚨 Emergency Sirens
- 💡 Warning Lights
- 📱 Instant Notifications

### Backup Mode
The alert system can operate even without internet connectivity.

---

# 💧 Intelligent Water Reuse

Instead of wasting floodwater, EchoPulse redirects and stores it safely.

### Water Quality Analysis
The system analyzes:

- pH
- Turbidity
- Temperature

### Results
- ✅ Clean water → reused for irrigation and services
- ⚠ Polluted water → safely discharged with alerts
